My Hobby - Sneakers

I enjoy following sneaker releases and learning about designs, collaborations, and the culture behind them.

Why I like sneakers

  • Creative designs and colorways
  • Different brands, styles, and technologies
  • Collecting pairs over time
  • Stories behind collaborations
  • Comfort and performance features

Favourite Brands

  • Nike
  • Adidas
  • New Balance
  • Jordan
  • ASICS
  • Puma

What I look for in a pair

  • Comfort for daily wear
  • Materials (leather, suede, mesh)
  • Design and color balance
  • Versatility with outfits
  • Price vs value

My style categories

  • Casual everyday sneakers
  • Streetwear / hype releases
  • Running-inspired lifestyle shoes
  • Basketball classics
  • Seasonal shoes (rain/winter)
